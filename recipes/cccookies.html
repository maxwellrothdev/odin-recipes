Chocolate Chip Cookies

The Chocolate Cookie of Chocolate Chip Cookies

That's right . . . the chocolate chipe cookie of all chocolate chip cookies. A chocolate chip cookie of which no other can possibly compete. Some would say this cookie could bring about world peace. Not just any chocolate chip cookie . . . THE chocolate chip cookie. Yes, it's that good!

Ingredients

  1. Flour 17 oz
  2. Baking soda 1 1/4 t
  3. Baking powder 1 1/2 t
  4. Sea salt 1 1/2 t
  5. Unsalted butter 2 1/2 sticks
  6. Brown sugar 10 oz
  7. Raw sugar 8 oz
  8. Eggs 2
  9. Vannila extract 2 t
  10. Chocolate chips 1 1/4 lb

Steps

  1. Combine flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Mix butter and sugars for 5 minutes.
  3. Add eggs one at a time.
  4. Stir in vanilla.
  5. Add dry ingredients and mix until just combined.
  6. Add chocolate chips.
  7. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  8. Using an ice cream scoop, place cookies on baking sheet at least 2 in apart.
  9. Sprinkle with sea salt.
  10. Bake until golden.
  11. Enjoy!
